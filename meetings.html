A Meeting Room Designed for Productivity

Looking for a professional yet peaceful setting for your next meeting? The meeting room at Birchwood Work Space is available to tenants and non-tenants, and offers the perfect blend of comfort and functionality. Seating up to six people around a spacious table, this well-equipped space features high-speed WiFi and a smart TV, ideal for presentations, video calls, and collaborative discussions.

Whether you're hosting a team meeting, a client presentation, or a brainstorming session, our meeting room provides a distraction-free environment just minutes from Caterham, Warlingham, Sanderstead, Whyteleafe, Oxted, Godstone and junction 6 of the M25. Escape the noise of traditional office spaces and bring your ideas to life in a setting that inspires focus and creativity.
