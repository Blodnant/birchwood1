Facilities

There is more to Birchwood than simply office spaces...

Bright and modern

Birchwood Work Space provides professional and modern office spaces that have been carefully designed with tenant well-being in mind. Our attention to detail and wonderful location will lift your mood, boost your energy levels and foster productivity and creativity, away from the pollution of the city.

Ergonomic furniture, abundance of natural light, efficient heating, and regular cleaning, ensure a comfortable and inspiring workspace. Stay seamlessly connected with high-speed WiFi for smooth collaboration and video calls. Surrounded by like-minded professionals, you’ll enjoy networking opportunities while maintaining a healthy work-life balance.


Gym

Birchwood Work Space boasts a modern and fully equipped gym that is for the exclusive use of Birchwood Work Space tenants. Equipment includes free weights, bike, treadmill and seated bike.


Country Life

Nestled in the serene countryside on the outskirts of Woldingham and Caterham, Birchwood Work Space offers a unique blend of rural tranquillity and modern convenience. Set amidst rolling fields and ancient woodlands, our workspace provides a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle, while keeping you well-connected to surrounding towns including Warlingham, Sanderstead, Whyteleafe, Oxted, and Godstone. Whether you’re going for a lunchtime run or taking a breath of fresh air between meetings in our idyllic setting, it's right outside your door.

Get in touch

We understand that every business is unique and will have specific requirements when it comes to their workspace. At Birchwood Work Space we will do everything we can to make our space work for you, so talk to us now to find out more and arrange a visit.
